About Labano Homes

Labano Homes is a web-based platform for property rentals and sales, we provide users with the best property search experience both online and offline by connecting them with legitimate and verified property owners. With the continuous rise in the property market in Nigeria, we would love to partner with owners of properties which include homes, houses, lands, shops, office spaces and other commercial properties, small, medium and large-scale real estate companies in Nigeria who desire to scale up the sales and rentals of their properties.
